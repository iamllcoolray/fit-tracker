# Workout Schedule

**Workout:** 60-120 mins
**Cardio:** 25 mins

**Sets:** 3-4
**Reps:** 8-12

**REMINDERS:**

* Stretch before and after every workout.
* Two workouts per large muscle group and one per small muscle group.

## Weekly Schedule

### Monday

* Upper Body

### Tuesday

* Lower Body

### Wednesday

* Rest/Cardio

### Thursday

* Upper Body

### Friday

* Lower Body

### Saturday

* Rest/Cardio

### Sunday

* Rest/Cardio
